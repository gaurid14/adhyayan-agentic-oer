📌 Forum Guidelines (read before posting)
    Violation of the same will lead to hiding/removal of your post.

  • Be respectful. No abusive, offensive, hateful, threatening, or harassing language.
  • Stay on topic. Post only course/learning-related questions, answers, and discussions. No spam.
  • No violence or threats. Any threat (even as a joke) will be hidden immediately.
  • No discrimination. Hate speech or targeting any religion/caste/race/gender/nationality is not allowed.
  • Keep it appropriate. No sexual, obscene, or vulgar content.
  • Protect privacy. Don’t share phone numbers, addresses, emails, passwords, OTPs, or anyone’s personal info.
  • No cheating. Don’t ask for or share full answers for graded exams/assignments; ask for explanations/help instead.
  • Write clearly. Use a clear title, add context, and mention what you tried. Low-effort/irrelevant replies may be removed.
  • Moderation. Content may be placed in Pending Review and hidden until approved. Repeated violations may lead to restrictions.
